Article text

DECLAN RICE has revealed Thomas Tuchel mesmerised the England players at half-time against Croatia. The Three Lions had been pegged back to 2-2 by their opponents just before the break. Captain Harry Kane had given England the lead twice at the Dallas Stadium in Arlington, Texas. But Martin Baturina and Peter Musa found the target for Croatia to equalise before half-time. After the break, England came out flying and Jude Bellingham re-established the lead within two minutes. They could have extended the lead on multiple occasions in the next 15 minutes after creating a flurry of chances.
