Thomas Tuchel's side got their campaign off to the perfect start with a 4-2 victory at the AT&T Stadium in Arlington, Texas, on Wednesday evening (BST). Ahead of kick-off, several supporters got into position early and attempted to hang their St George's flags, a tradition England fans are known for at major tournaments. However, as many of these were covering official FIFA hoardings, something that the governing body have banned, stewards intervened and forced the fans to remove them. According to The Telegraph, they threatened to confiscate the flags if they weren't taken down, a stance that was said to leave several supporters 'appalled'. One fan from London called Dan, who had an 8ft x 5ft Arsenal and England flag, said: 'For the amount of money we have paid it is a joke. But what can you do?
