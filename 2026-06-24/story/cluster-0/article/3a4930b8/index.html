Frustrated England held to World Cup stalemate with Ghana

Article text

England were held to a frustrating goalless draw by Ghana, as a compact Ghana back line stifled their opportunities. The Three Lions chalked up 19 shots but were contained by a deep and well structured Ghanaian defence. They only hit the target three times despite managing 78.8 per cent possession. It was the antithesis of the thrilling 4-2 victory over Croatia in England’s first game at this year’s World Cup. The draw leaves England sitting at the top of the Group L table. Ghana are level on four points but have an inferior goal difference.
