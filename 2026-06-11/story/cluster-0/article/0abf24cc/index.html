England vs Costa Rica recap: Thomas Tuchel has extra friendly arranged with new selection dilemma England came away victorious in their final World Cup warm-up match against Costa Rica as Declan Rice, Anthony Gordon and Ollie Watkins fired Thomas Tuchel's side to a win England dispatched Costa Rica 3-0 in their final World Cup warm-up match in Orlando. They were slow to start after a thunderstorm delayed kick-off by an hour, but soon found themselves in the ascendancy against their opponents. Declan Rice opened the scoring after good work from Anthony Gordon in the first half, as the Three Lions struggled to capitalise on their ascendancy. It was Gordon who stepped up to double England's lead from the spot in the second period, with Harry Kane off the field. Eberechi Eze's strike found an elbow and the Barcelona star fired in an unstoppable penalty. Ollie Watkins showed exactly why he was on the plane by tapping in after Aston Villa team-mate Morgan Rogers had his shot from distance saved. The goal capped off a dominant outing that still tested Tuchel's side in a few areas. The German coach also had a boost in Bukayo Saka coming off the bench after Tuchel had highlighted an injury concern for the Arsenal star.
