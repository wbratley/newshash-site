Stokes and Atkinson dropped for second New Zealand Test after curfew breach; Root named interim captain

Unbiased summary

England Test captain Ben Stokes and fast bowler Gus Atkinson have been excluded from the 15-man squad for the second Test against New Zealand at The Oval, starting 17 June, following a curfew breach in the early hours of Monday morning after England's victory in the first Test at Lord's. The pair were present at the Rex Rooms in Chelsea when an altercation occurred involving Saracens rugby player Totoa Auvaa. Both the ECB and the independent Cricket Regulator are investigating the incident. No formal disciplinary action has been announced. Joe Root has been named interim captain, with vice-captain Harry Brook not stepping up. Jofra Archer and Jordan Cox have been added to the squad. Stokes is reported to be considering his future, though those fears are said to have eased.
