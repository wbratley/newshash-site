Boy, 14, charged with murder after body believed to be missing girl Lilly found in Blaina, south Wales

Unbiased summary

A 14-year-old boy from the Blaenau Gwent area has been charged with murder following the discovery of a body in the Duffryn Park area of Blaina, south Wales, on 22 June. The body is believed, pending formal identification, to be that of Lilly, a 14-year-old girl who was reported missing after last being seen on Blaina High Street at approximately 6.50pm on 20 June. The boy, described by police as white British, cannot be named for legal reasons and was remanded in custody to appear at Newport Magistrates Court on 26 June. Parts of Pilgrims Park were cordoned off and floral tributes were left near the scene. Police and the Crown Prosecution Service both urged the public to avoid online commentary that could prejudice proceedings.
