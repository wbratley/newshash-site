Full list of Labour MPs to nominate Andy Burnham – including 18 members of Starmer’s cabinet

Article text

Full list of Labour MPs to nominate Andy Burnham – including 18 members of Starmer’s cabinet Ed Miliband, Chief Whip Jonathan Reynolds and Chief Secretary Darren Jones are among those who have put their names behind Mr Burnham - Bookmark - CommentsGo to comments Andy Burnham has garnered significant support for the Labour leadership, with 322 MPs, including several senior Cabinet ministers, putting his name forward. Among those backing Mr Burnham are Chancellor Rachel Reeves, Deputy Prime Minister David Lammy, and Foreign Secretary Yvette Cooper. In total, 18 members of Sir Keir Starmer’s Cabinet have publicly endorsed his bid, including Energy and Net Zero Secretary Ed Miliband, Chief Whip Jonathan Reynolds, and the staunchly loyal Chief Secretary Darren Jones. Further high-profile nominations include Angela Rayner and current deputy leader Lucy Powell. One-time potential challengers Al Carns and Wes Streeting have also lent their support, alongside former transport secretary Louise Haigh and Dame Emily Thornberry, who chairs the Foreign Affairs select committee. However, Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood and Housing Secretary Steve Reed are notable Cabinet figures who have yet to nominate Mr Burnham, though they still have time to do so.
