Andy Burnham Becomes New Labour Leader

Unbiased summary

Andy Burnham has been confirmed as the new leader of the Labour Party, succeeding Keir Starmer. He won the support of 349 Labour MPs and will become the UK's next prime minister on Monday. Burnham has promised to lead a united Labour government and to undo the economic legacy of Margaret Thatcher. He has also pledged to devolve more power to communities and to support small businesses.
