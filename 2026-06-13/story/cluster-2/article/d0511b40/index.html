King's Birthday Honours List 2026 in full: Every person who received recognition from the monarch The list has been unveiled ahead of the King's Birthday Parade on Saturday June 13th Don't Miss Most Read The release of the King’s Birthday Honours List 2026 provides an opportunity to recognise outstanding service and contribution across British society. Close to 1,200 individuals across the United Kingdom have been recognised, celebrating exceptional contributions to British society and communities nationwide. Rugby league legend Kevin Sinfield stands among the most prominent recipients, receiving a knighthood for his distinguished sporting career and tireless efforts raising funds for Motor Neurone Disease research and affected families. Dame Helen Mirren has been elevated to Companion of Honour, one of the most prestigious distinctions in the honours system. Half a dozen Lionesses who triumphed at the European Championship also feature prominently in this year's list, alongside celebrated names from music, literature and business. Below GB News takes a look at the full list of individuals honoured.
