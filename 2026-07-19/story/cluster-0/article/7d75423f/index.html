England vs France LIVE: Saka hat-trick bags third place in INSANE 6-4 World Cup bronze final England's dream of ending 60 years of hurt were dashed by Argentina on Wednesday night, but they still have the chance to record their best ever finish on foreign soil England have absolutely blown France away in the World Cup match nobody wants to play. Declan Rice scored a screamer after just three minutes to lift the mood for England, who would record the nation's greatest ever finish on foreign soil with a win. Ezri Konsa scored from a Rice corner before Bukayo Saka plundered a brace to give England a 4-0 half-time lead. Kylian Mbappe provided an immediate response for a much-changed France team just minutes after the restart to restore from French pride. Victory for England would also go some way to easing the pain following the meek World Cup semi-final loss to Argentina in midweek, the manner of which has left Thomas Tuchel under pressure. Th England head coach's name was booed by supporters in the stadium when his name was read out pre-match. Rice is captaining the team with Harry Kane and Jude Bellingham among the big names on the bench absence, while Kobbie Mainoo misses out due to injury.
