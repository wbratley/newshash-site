Man named after death at Peak District stone circle during summer solstice event; one arrested on suspicion of murder

Unbiased summary

Isaac Clare-Watts, 26, from Nottingham, was found dead at the Nine Ladies Stone Circle in Stanton Lees, Derbyshire, at approximately 1:38pm on Monday 23 June. Emergency services attended and he was pronounced dead at the scene. Derbyshire Police, with the East Midlands Special Operations Unit leading the investigation, named him on Wednesday. A 41-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of murder and remains in custody. The incident occurred during a summer solstice gathering, with police stating a number of people were present at the site between 19 and 23 June. Detective Inspector Tony Owen made an urgent public appeal for witnesses, video footage, and dashcam recordings. A major incident public portal has been established. The victim's family has been informed and is being supported by specialist officers.
