Toronto Festival Shooting Leaves 2 Dead, 4 Injured

Unbiased summary

A shooting occurred near a Toronto street festival, killing two men and wounding four others. The incident involved an exchange of gunfire between two individuals, with police recovering two firearms. No suspects have been arrested, and investigations are ongoing. The festival, which celebrates Latin American culture, was attended by thousands of people, and a large police presence remains in the area.
