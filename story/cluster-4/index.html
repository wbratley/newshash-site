US political commentators Hasan Piker and Cenk Uygur barred from UK after electronic travel authorisations cancelled by Home Office

Unbiased summary

Two American political commentators, Hasan Piker and Cenk Uygur, were denied entry to the United Kingdom after the Home Office cancelled their Electronic Travel Authorisations (ETAs). Both had been scheduled to speak at events connected to SXSW London. The specific reasons cited by the Home Office have not been fully disclosed publicly, though reports link the decision to concerns including antisemitism allegations. The bans drew criticism from free speech advocates and political figures including Green Party deputy leader Zack Polanski, who described the decision negatively. Both Piker and Uygur are known as left-leaning commentators in the United States, with Uygur hosting The Young Turks and Piker being a prominent online political streamer.
