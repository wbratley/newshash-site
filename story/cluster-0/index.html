England Advances to World Cup Semi-Finals

Unbiased summary

England defeated Norway 2-1 in extra time, with Jude Bellingham scoring twice. The match was closely contested, with Norway taking the lead in the first half through Andreas Schjelderup's goal. England equalized just before halftime, and Bellingham scored the winning goal in extra time. The victory sends England to the semi-finals, where they will face either Argentina or Switzerland.
