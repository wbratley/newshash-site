Andy Burnham Becomes UK Prime Minister

Unbiased summary

Andy Burnham has become the UK's new Prime Minister, succeeding Sir Keir Starmer. He has announced his cabinet, including John Healey as Chancellor and Ed Miliband as Foreign Secretary. Burnham has pledged to address the cost of living crisis and end rough sleeping, and has spoken with world leaders, including US President Donald Trump. He has also outlined plans for a new economic model and devolution of power to regional authorities.
