Jude Bellingham scores twice in just 98 seconds against Mexico as fans say ‘England have finally arrived at World Cup’

Article text

JUDE BELLINGHAM bagged two goals in just 98 seconds to give England a dream start in their World Cup win against Mexico. The Three Lions were up against it ahead of their Last-16 showdown at the Azteca as talk around a daunting atmosphere and high altitude dominated the build-up. But England quickly put those negative headlines to bed as Bellingham inspired a heroic 3-2 win with ten men. After a quiet opening to the game, Real Madrid man Bellingham sparked England into life with a brilliant diving header to put them ahead in the 36th minute. And less than two minutes later he was at the double after tapping home from Harry Kane‘s cross. Mexico did pull one back from Julian Quinones to make it 2-1 just before half time.
