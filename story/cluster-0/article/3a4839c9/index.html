World Cup halftime show reaction: Wayne Rooney slams 'flat' show but Shakira's BGT guests shine Wayne Rooney has hit out at the FIFA World Cup 2026 halftime performances from the likes of K-pop stars BTS as well as Shakira, Justin Bieber and Burna Boy The FIFA World Cup 2026 halftime performance delighted thousands – but not Wayne Rooney. The former footballer-turned-pundit revealed that he believed the performances were "flat". "Mine was when it finished," he said of his favourite part. Rooney added: "I like a lot of those artists but I thought it was crap. I did, honestly, it just didn't get me going, I wanted the football to come back on." He later explained that he believed Justin Bieber's performance was "flat". "I liked Burna Boy, I like Bieber, I like Shakira, but that was too flat," he fumed. But fans at home instantly took to social media to disagree with Rooney's comments. Instead, it was widely said that the performance was "better" than the first half of the match. So much so that even Oasis rocker Liam Gallagher waded in, praising Justin Bieber, who performed a rendition of his track Everything Hallelujah. But Liam's comments did come with a caveat. While he said Justin "rules", he later shared his brutally honest thoughts. "This is like a bad trip," he said, adding: "It's a good job I've got my spiritual socks on or that halftime entertainment could have tipped me over the edge." Madonna, BTS, Shakira featuring Burna Boy and Bieber performed during the 11-minute performance, a historic first for the World Cup.
