'Have to be honest, there's so much frustration,' Barry said in his final half-time address. 'I'm a little bit emotional, I can't find the words about how proud I am of those players. 'We're playing a game with broken hearts, 11 lads with broken hearts. France have not conceded four goals in a single half since their European Championship qualification match against Yugoslavia in 1968.
