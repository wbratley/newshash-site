Cristiano Ronaldo breaks down in tears as he wears Diogo Jota shirt in emotional tribute on first anniversary of death

Article text

CRISTIANO RONALDO broke down in tears as he wore a replica Diogo Jota shirt following Portugal’s dramatic World Cup win. The five-time Ballon d’Or winner paid an emotional tribute to his teammate on the one-year anniversary of his death. CR7 was spotted wearing the number 21 Portugal shirt following their controversial 2-1 last 32 win over Croatia. Ronaldo was also seen tearing up on the pitch following the final whistle in Toronto, Canada. It comes exactly a year after Jota was killed in a car crash in Spain on July 3rd, last year. The two were close friends on the international stage, and also off the pitch. A photograph of Jota, who died in a car crash last summer, had also been shown on the giant screens in BMO Field after the Portuguese anthem was played pre-match.
