Yet those expecting the former champion to quietly fade away underestimated the competitive instinct that has defined her career. Williams gradually found her timing, serving with greater authority and unleashing flashes of the thunderous hitting that once made her virtually untouchable on grass. The second set became vintage Serena in spirit if not always execution.
