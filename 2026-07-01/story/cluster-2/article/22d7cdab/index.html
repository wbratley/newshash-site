Mexico vs Ecuador DELAYED just minutes before kick-off as weather wreaks havoc on World Cup clash

THE World Cup last-32 clash between Mexico and Ecuador has been delayed. Thunderstorms and lightning in the Mexico City area forced officials to scrap plans to begin at 7pm local time (2am UK). Fans gathered at the iconic Azteca – but were kept waiting for the action to begin. Fifa released a statement on the decision. It read: “Due to adverse weather conditions in Mexico City, including risk caused by lightning in the vicinity of the stadium, the kick-off of the FIFA World Cup round of 32 match between Mexico and Ecuador has been delayed. “FIFA will follow the safety protocols established by the local authorities, and the match will start as soon as it is safe to do so.
