England player ratings as 10/10 Jude Bellingham fires Three Lions into quarter-finals

Article text

England are into the quarter-finals of the World Cup after one of the most dramatic nights in their history. The Three Lions overcame Mexico 3-2 in the cauldron of the Azteca thanks to two goals from Jude Bellingham and a Harry Kane penalty. But England were forced to play almost the entire second half with 10 men after Jarell Quansah so red for a horror tackle. Bellingham's first-half brace put England 2-0 up before Julian Quinones pulled one back for Mexico. Kane scored from the spot after Quansah's red card to restore the two-goal lead. Raul Jimenez then also scored from 12 yards and England clung on for more than half an hour to book a place in the last eight against Norway. Here's how the players rated in Mexico City...
