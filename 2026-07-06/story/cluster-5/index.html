Australian PM Albanese apologises after making sexual comments about Kylie Minogue during podcast interview

Unbiased summary

Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese appeared on the Bush Deep podcast with comedian Nikki Osborne, where he was asked to play a 'shag, marry or date' game involving Kylie Minogue, Nicole Kidman, and Rhonda Burchmore. After initial reluctance citing his recent marriage, Albanese said 'Kylie, clearly' and confirmed 'all of the above' when asked if he meant all three categories. The interview, recorded at the PM's official Canberra residence, also included a question about his sex life with his wife. Following weekend criticism from MPs across the political spectrum, Albanese issued a one-line statement on Monday: 'I apologise unequivocally for the comments.' He was in Fiji at the time of the apology for diplomatic meetings.
