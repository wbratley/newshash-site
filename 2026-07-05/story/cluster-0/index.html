Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce marry at Madison Square Garden in Fourth of July weekend ceremony with Dior wedding attire

Unbiased summary

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce married at Madison Square Garden in New York City on Friday, 4 July 2025, confirmed by Swift's publicist. The venue displayed 'JUST&T MARRIED' on giant screens at 7:30pm. Around 1,000 guests attended. Swift wore a bespoke Dior haute couture gown designed by Jonathan Anderson; Kelce also wore Dior. Shoes were by Christian Louboutin and Swift's jewellery by Cartier. Actor Adam Sandler officiated. Swift's brother Austin served as her Man of Honour; Jason Kelce was Best Man. Swift reportedly walked down the aisle to an instrumental arrangement of her song Love Story. Reception performances included Paul McCartney and Stevie Nicks. Guests described vows as personal and humorous. Decor transformed MSG into a garden-themed space with pink and green drapery and foliage.
