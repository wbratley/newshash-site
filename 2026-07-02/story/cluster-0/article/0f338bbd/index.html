Belgium 3 Senegal 2: Red Devils win World Cup tie with last-minute penalty after VAR drama as they fight from 2-0 down

Article text

YOURI TIELEMANS struck a dramatic and controversial extra-time penalty for Belgium to completely break Senegal hearts. Lamine Camara was controversially adjudged to have fouled the Aston Villa playmaker with only ONE minute left of the gruelling extra-time period. Crystal Palace winger Ismaila Sarr thought he had sealed Senegal’s place in the last-16 with a truly wonderful finish earlier in the match. The 28-year-old speedster blasted home six minutes after the interval to put the African underdogs 2-0 in front. He elegantly brought down a long ball from Moussa Niakhaté on his chest before rifling past ex-Chelsea keeper Thibaut Courtois. The shock second goal came after Habib Diarra had initially put Senegal into a deserved 25th-minute lead. The costly blunder allowed a charging Tielemans to arrive at the back post and head home to trigger absolute bedlam.
