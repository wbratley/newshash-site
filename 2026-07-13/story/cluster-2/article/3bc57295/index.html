Harry Kane fires huge warning to Argentina ahead of England World Cup semi as Lionel Messi braces for ‘special’ battle

Article text

HARRY KANE vows England WILL improve ahead of Wednesday’s World Cup semi-final showdown with bitter rivals Argentina in Atlanta. And superstar Lionel Messi is relishing a “special” first meeting with Thomas Tuchel’s “powerhouse” Three Lions. Kane knows England were lucky to seal a 2-1 comeback extra-time win over Norway following Jude Bellingham’s double. Now the skipper is demanding more from his team-mates to overcome Messi’s 2022 reigning champions to fire his country to a first World Cup final in 60 years. Asked how much Tuchel’s men can improve, Kane, 32, said: “I know this is going to be a topic from now until the next game and we know we can improve. We know we can play better. “But ultimately, we are in a semi-final of the World Cup and that has not always been the case for this national team so we have to enjoy it.
