Trump threw out the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action, the Obama-era Iran nuclear deal, during his first term. 'The Leaders of the Region have, for the first time, found a President who can help them achieve real Peace,' the President continued. 'With the opening of the Strait upon the signing of the Deal on Friday, for purposes of mine removal, oil will flow on both ends again for the Region, and the World!'
