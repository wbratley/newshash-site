Senior officers were under pressure to explain why they so publicly ruled out terror or a political motivation. The Mail has learnt that items of varying political ideology, including Russian material, have been found at the suspect's home and on electronic devices. But investigators do not believe there is any evidence of hostile state involvement. Counter terrorism police are investigating whether the 'lone wolf' targeted Ms Widdecombe due to her political views, which she frequently expressed during regular appearances on TV and radio defending Reform as the party's immigration and justice spokesman.
