Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce officially married in front of swooning Hollywood elite A statement from Swift's publicist revealed a surprising detail about the nature of the ceremony Don't Miss Most Read Latest Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have officially been married, the singer's publicist has confirmed. Tree Paine confirmed in a statement on Friday night the A-list couple had finally tied the knot - and outlined a series of details about their glitzy nuptials. They did not have bridesmaids or groomsmen, Ms Paine's statement said. Instead, Taylor's brother Austin served as the country superstar's "man of honour". Jason Kelce, Travis's brother, was the KC Chiefs tight end's best man. The statement also revealed one surprising detail: Comedy actor Adam Sandler officiated the ceremony. Though it stopped short of confirming the exact time the duo were wed. As the news emerged, billboards outside New York City's Madison Square Garden were illuminated with the message "JusT&T married!". Jay-Z, Steven Spielberg, Gigi Hadid, Bradley Cooper and Hugh Grant were among the 1,000 attendees spotted piling into "MSG" earlier on Friday. NFL stars including San Francisco 49ers fullback Kyle Juszczyk, LA Rams quarterback Matthew Stafford and more also turned up for Kelce's big day. Legions of fans and wide-eyed journalists gathered outside to catch a glimpse of any big-name guests.
