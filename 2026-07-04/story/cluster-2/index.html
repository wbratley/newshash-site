Argentina beat Cape Verde 3-2 after extra time in World Cup Round of 32, advancing to face Egypt in Atlanta

Unbiased summary

Argentina defeated Cape Verde 3-2 after extra time in a World Cup Round of 32 match at Hard Rock Stadium, Miami. Lionel Messi opened the scoring in the 29th minute. Cape Verde equalised through Deroy Duarte in the 59th minute, taking the game to extra time at 1-1. Lisandro Martínez put Argentina ahead two minutes into extra time, but Sidny Lopes Cabral levelled again at 2-2 with a long-range curling strike in the 102nd minute. Argentina scored the decisive third goal when Cristian Romero's header deflected in off defender Diney Borges. Cape Verde, ranked 67th in the world and making their World Cup debut, had also drawn with Spain and Uruguay in the group stage. Argentina advance to play Egypt in the Round of 16 in Atlanta.
