Two Romanian men jailed for combined 20 years for knife attack on Iran International journalist Pouria Zeraati in London

Unbiased summary

Nandito Badea, 21, and George Stana, 25, both Romanian nationals, were sentenced at the Old Bailey on Friday after being convicted of wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm. They attacked Iran International journalist Pouria Zeraati outside his Wimbledon home on 29 March 2024, stabbing him three times in the thigh. Stana received 12 years and Badea eight years. Mrs Justice Cheema-Grubb stated she was satisfied the attack was carried out on behalf of the Iranian state, noting Zeraati was a known critic of the Tehran regime and had previously received threats. A third man, David Andrei, who participated in the attack, remains in Romania and was not tried. Prosecutors said the attack followed extensive surveillance and was ordered by a third party acting on behalf of Iran.
